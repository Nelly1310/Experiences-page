UWASA is a group of students of African origin who hope to promote African culture and interaction. As the Events Coordinator for the University of Waterloo African Students Association (UWASA), I organize engaging events that celebrate African culture and foster community among students. From conceptualizing themes to managing logistics, this role allows me to merge creativity and leadership, creating meaningful experiences that highlight African heritage.

Technical Director

The AfroXDance club is aimed at bridging the gap between cultural and traditional African dance styles and other genres of dance: hip-hop, contemporary, jazz, salsa, etc. In my role as the Technical Director of AfroXdance, I oversee the production aspects of performances, ensuring smooth choreography execution, music selection, and visual aesthetics. This position enables me to blend my passion for dance with organizational skills, delivering captivating performances that reflect cultural richness and energy.
